Welcome to ChatAway

A friendly space for students to join groups & clubs, access dedicated chatrooms, and help each other with timely updates, Q&A, and resources.

Create or join a group, pick a channel, and start collaborating real-time messages, pinned info, and notifications keep everyone in sync.

Tip: You can follow multiple groups at once and switch between channels without losing your place.
